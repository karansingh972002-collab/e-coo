## 🚀 How to Use:

1. **Browse Products**: Scroll through the homepage
2. **Filter**: Click category buttons to filter products
3. **Sort**: Use the dropdown to sort by price or rating
4. **View Details**: Click any product card
5. **Add to Cart**: Click "ADD TO CART" button
6. **View Cart**: Click the cart icon (🛒) in the header
7. **Checkout**: Click "PROCEED TO CHECKOUT" button
8. **Complete Order**: Fill out the form and click "PLACE ORDER"

## 🔧 Commands:
